Lemonade

Let's make a summer classic! Lemonade is a great choice for this season.

Ingredients

  • 4 lemons
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 6 cups cold water

Steps

  1. Cut the lemons in half
  2. Scoop the pulp into a blender
  3. Add half of the rinds along with sugar, vanilla extract, and 2 cups of water
  4. Blend until smooth
  5. Strain
